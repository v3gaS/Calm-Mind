# Disclaimer

**Read this before using CalmMind.**

## Not a medical device

CalmMind is a **wellness and relaxation tool**, not a medical device, diagnostic
system, or substitute for professional healthcare, psychotherapy, or emergency
services.

- UI labels such as “brain band,” “Hz,” or “evidence” describe **target audio
  frequencies** and **research-informed presets**, not live EEG or clinical outcomes.
- **BLS / “Calm Down”** presets provide bilateral tones for self-regulation; they are
  **not** clinical EMDR therapy unless delivered by a qualified clinician in an
  appropriate setting.
- If you have epilepsy, a seizure disorder, severe psychiatric symptoms, or are
  unsure whether auditory stimulation is safe for you, **consult a qualified
  professional** before use.

## No guaranteed outcomes

Individual responses to binaural beats, isochronic tones, and ambient sound vary.
The authors make **no warranty** of therapeutic benefit, sleep improvement, or
stress reduction.

## Safe use

- Use **comfortable volume**; remove headphones if you feel dizzy, nauseous, or pained.
- **Do not use** while driving, operating machinery, or in situations requiring full alertness.
- **Headphones** are recommended for binaural and bilateral content; monaural/isochronic
  content may work on speakers per preset hints in the app.
