# Research notes (background only)

Files in this folder summarize **third-party literature and product research** used
to inform CalmMind sound types and copy. They are **not** clinical trial results for
this application.

## Important limitations

| Topic | How to interpret |
|-------|------------------|
| Binaural / isochronic / monaural beats | Mechanisms and citations are **educational**; individual efficacy varies. |
| Solfeggio, neuroacoustic, vibroacoustic | Often **theoretical or commercial** framing in sources — not FDA/EMA validated claims. |
| EMDR / BLS | App presets are **self-regulation aids**, not licensed EMDR treatment. |
| HRV / coherence | Modulation targets breathing pace; **not** live HRV measurement from this app. |

## External links

Research markdown may link to PMC, Healthline, commercial wellness sites, etc.
Those sites are **independent**; link rot and bias may apply. Verify claims with
primary literature before clinical or product claims.

## Product mapping

Evidence-tagged **protocol presets** in the shipped app live in
[`client/js/protocols.js`](../../client/js/protocols.js). Tags such as `evidence`
in code indicate **design intent** (e.g. published frequency ranges), not a guarantee
of outcome for this build.
